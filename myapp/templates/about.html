What is FERTPPM?

As humans, considering that vegetables are the main source of food and survival, figuring out ways to increase the quality is the utmost priority. In addition, alot of countries, especially in Africa relies on export of produce to make capital.FERTPPM is a Fertigation system used in agriculture and horticulture that combines fertilization and irrigation technology to optimize Your crop yield. It focuses on the observation and maintainance of produce in a monitored environment which will later impact the quantity and quality

Why FERTPPM?

Prior to starting, we were in a pool of all these project suggestions that sounded really great as portfolio projects until Benson who is one of the members of the team behind its creation sugested it. It was a work in progress app but running in a desktop environment. Taking it up and reprogramming it into a web app has been a challenge and a good experience. It really helpedthat we learnt more from each other in both front and backend aspects
