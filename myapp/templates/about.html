    • What is FERTPPM?

    As humans, considering that vegetables are the main source of food and survival, figuring out ways to increase the quality is the utmost priority. In addition, a lot of countries, especially in Africa, rely on the export of produce to generate capital. FERTPPM is a Fertigation system used in agriculture and horticulture that combines fertilizer and irrigation technology to optimize your crop yield. It focuses on the observation and maintenance of produce in a monitored environment, which will later impact the quantity and quality.

    Why FERTPPM?

    Prior to starting, we were in a pool of all these project suggestions that sounded really great as portfolio projects until Benson, who is one of the members of the team behind its creation, suggested it. It was a work-in-progress app but running in a desktop environment. Taking it up and reprogramming it into a web app has been a challenge and a good experience. It really helped that we learned more from each other in both frontend and backend aspects.

    Future FERTPPM?

    As of the time of writing up this section of the application, we both feel like there is so much room for improvements and tweaks. We have so many ideas and visions of a fully functional marketable app that, when combined together, are the motivation behind creating this app that can hopefully one day be put into a business setup and generate revenue.
